About Women Fortune Tech

Women Fortune Tech is Pakistan’s premier Vernacular Upskilling platform, empowering women to unlock their potential in the digital world. We provide access to top-tier skill-building programs in regional languages, helping women overcome barriers to success.

Founder & CEO

Hamid Shinwari — CEO & Founder, Women Fortune Tech

The CEO of Women Fortune Tech is a passionate advocate for women’s empowerment and digital inclusion. She founded Pakistan’s premier Vernacular Upskilling Platform, providing accessible, job-ready training programs in regional languages.

Her leadership focuses on equipping women with in-demand digital skills, fostering confidence and economic independence.

Works across HR, training initiatives and community development.
